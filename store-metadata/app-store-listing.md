## Description

Build lasting habits with the power of AI coaching.

AI Habit Coach helps you create, track, and stick to daily habits with personalized AI insights that adapt to your behavior. Whether you want to improve your health, build mindfulness, boost productivity, or develop new skills — our AI coach keeps you motivated and on track.

HOW IT WORKS
- Set your goals and pick habits that matter to you
- Track daily completions and build streaks
- Get AI-powered weekly insights that analyze your patterns
- Receive smart reminders that adapt to your schedule

KEY FEATURES

Personalized AI Coaching
Your AI coach generates weekly analyses of your habit patterns, identifies trends, and provides actionable recommendations tailored to your progress.

Smart Habit Tracking
Track daily habits across categories like health, fitness, mindfulness, learning, and productivity. Monitor your streaks and celebrate milestones.

Habit Stacking (Premium)
Chain habits together for maximum impact. Get AI-suggested habit stacks based on your existing routines.

Adaptive Reminders
Set custom reminder times for each habit. Smart nudges help you stay on track if habits remain incomplete.

Progress Visualization
Beautiful activity heatmaps, completion rate charts, and weekly progress reports help you see your growth over time.
